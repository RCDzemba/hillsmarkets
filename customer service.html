Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the "Your Orders" section in your account. Alternatively, you can use the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email. If you're having trouble tracking your order, please contact our customer service team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in their original condition. Some items like electronics may have different return windows. To start a return, visit the "Returns & Refunds" section in your account or contact our customer service team for assistance.

How can I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, as long as it hasn't entered the shipping process. Visit the "Your Orders" section in your account to make changes. If the option to cancel is unavailable, your order is already being prepared for shipment.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within South Africa. We're working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations in the future. Please check back for updates on our international shipping services.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team through the contact form on this page, by phone at 0800 123 456, or via email at support@hillsmarket.co.za. Our support hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.
